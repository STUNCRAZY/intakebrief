This is what happens after a prospective client submits an IntakeBrief form.

They enter a short, non-confidential description and accept the required notices.

The system delivers the lead to the firm, identifies the likely matter, and sends the client a response that matches the inquiry.

The client chooses an open consultation time, and IntakeBrief holds it while Stripe hosts the fifty-dollar test checkout.

After payment, the browser waits for signed webhook verification.

The confirmation email closes the loop: qualified lead, scheduled consultation, and verified deposit.
